Kentville

A place to live.

Home

Kentville, Nova Scotia is a major hub in the Annapolis Valley Region. Located about two hours from Halifax, Kentville is a small town famously known for its beautiful infrastructure and for being one of the richest agricultural towns in North America.
Kentville is also known for its apple cultivation and the Apple Blossom festival held every year in the month of May to celebrate the start of the season for apples, since it is the main agricultural produce of the region.
